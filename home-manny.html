Our Student's Experiences

Flight training should be life time experience

Flying Academy is International Flight Training Center
EASA & FAA approved flight school

Flying Academy is one of the international leaders in flight training. For more than three decades, we have been preparing pilots for various airlines of the world according to the highest industry standards. We are specialized in ab-initio flight training. However, we offer full spectrum of courses.

Flying Academy is a flight school authorized by the FAA (Federal Aviation Administration) (https://www.faa.gov/) as well as for EASA (European Aviation Safety Agency) (https://www.easa.europa.eu/). The academy’s highest priority is to provide a safe flying environment to all the future pilots. We believe in providing the best flight experience for your success in future pilot career. Whether based in Prague, Brno, Miami or Los Angeles, Flying Academy’s most important goal is to satisfy the customers.

Flight training experience, innovations

12 years of experience of Flying Academy in Europe and even 30 years of experience accumulated in the Miami base, the Academy is able to provide excellent training through experienced pilots worldwide. This real-life pilot experience is reflected in the exceptional training that is very practically oriented and provides hands-on learning experiences. It is the academy’s goal to create an interactive and diverse learning environment. We strongly focus on innovations which keeps us ahead of the competition not only by global presence but also in technology.

Our students have the availability of using the latest state of the art equipment for studying: the ATPL question bank, Bristol ground school CBT, Wifi-equipped classrooms, video materials and even subsidized iPad for students participating in the programs ATPL modular, 0-CPL, as well as 0-ATPL. Changes in the industry are immediately reflected on the curricula to ensure that all learning materials stay current and Flying Academy continues to provide the best training opportunities to its customers.
