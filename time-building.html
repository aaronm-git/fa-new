Flying Academy Europe boasts:

  • Flexible discount system. Rental fee decreases depending on the amount of hours that you fly.
  • Perfect location for international flying. Czech Republic is located in the heart of Europe, making European Union regions easily reachable.
  • Diverse fleet selection. Click here to check aircraft availability.
  • Tailor-made solutions for each trainee, from affordable leisure flying, or VIP service packages, to commercial pilots solutions.
  • Accessibility of training bases in Prague and Brno. Our bases are located at international airports, within the city line.
  • Safety is our main priority! All aircrafts receive regular maintenance checks and on duty instructors to advise flight planning, weather, etc.
  • Accommodation for foreign trainees in Flying Academy’s student houses. Read more about accommodation in the Czech Republic.
  • Visa support. We'll assist our students with all Visa related matters.
  • Low expenses. Our landing and communication fees are within the lowest in Europe.
  • Responsive Customer Service and support throughout your study within Flying Academy.
